Alice's potato bites

  • 5 Min - Preparation time
  • 5 Min - Cook time
  • 10 Min - Total
  • 2 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 litre of cooking oil
  • 2 teaspoon of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of red chilli
  • 100g all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp flakes
  • 10 medium sized potatoes

DIRECTIONS

1. first wash and boil the potatoes for about 10 minutes:
2. peel the potatoes and grate them: In a bowl ...

  • oil the cooking pan with.
  • add garlic 2 tablespoon grated.
  • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until the garlic turn golden brown.
  • Stir in 1 teaspoon of red chilli.
  • add 3 tablespoon of flakes.
  • stir the mixture.
  • add 2 cups of water.
  • bring it to boil.
  • Pour 100 grams of baking flour to the mixture.
3cooling: let it cool completely.
  • Add the grated potatoes to the dough.
  • Add 3 tablespoon of corn flour.
  • Using your hands mix the ingredients well.
  • after the mixture have fully mixed,grease your hands with cooking oil.
  • Roll and shape them into small balls.
  • 4. Frying: fry the balls into a frying pan on a medium frame until they turn brown.

    This is our favorite recipe at home. It's our family favorite snack! We hope you'll find that this recipe is delicious and also easy to follow; it's a piece of pie!
